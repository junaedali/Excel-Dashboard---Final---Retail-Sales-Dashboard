## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

Created KPI cards to show the most important business metrics, including Sales, Profit, Quantity, Number of Orders, and Profit Percentage.

Each KPI card includes a mini trend line and year-over-year growth indicator to quickly understand business performance.

### Sales vs Profit Analysis

Created a monthly Sales vs Profit chart to compare revenue and profitability trends across different months.

This helps identify which months generated higher sales and how profit changed over time.

### Sales by District

Used a Bangladesh map visualization to show sales performance by district.

This helps understand geographical sales distribution and identify strong or weak sales areas.

### Category Wise Analysis

Created a category-wise chart to compare performance across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.

This section helps understand which product category contributes more to overall business performance.

### Top 5 Sub-Categories

Created a horizontal bar chart to identify the top-performing sub-categories.

Top 5 sub-categories shown in the dashboard:

- Phones
- Chairs
- Storage
- Tables
- Binders

### Category Wise Sales

Created a donut chart to show category-wise sales contribution percentage.

This helps compare the sales share of different product categories.

## Interactive Filters

Added slicers to make the dashboard interactive and easy to analyze.

Filters used:

- Region
- Segment
- Year

Users can filter the dashboard by region, customer segment, and year to view specific insights.
